Spending Calendar — Three Directions

The same app, three souls. Same data (June 2026), same screens. The non-negotiables hold across all three (money is the only loud color, one giant hero number, warm-not-cold). What changes is how funky we get. Tap a direction.

Direction A · Type as art
The Specimen
Acanski / Ujjo foundry energy: the dollar amount is the artwork. Gigantic italic Fraunces, set like a typeface showing off. One electric tile quarantines the only saturated color; acid-lime marks today. Editorial, confident, a little cheeky.
Direction B · Emotion-first
The Landscape
The Quake move (kept — you liked the graph): a month of daily spend as a warm aubergine→coral→peach waveform with a glowing center seam. Dry data becomes a sunset.
Direction C · Print / riso
The Broadsheet
atozsurfing's hairline-ruled ledger as a financial print zine: a newspaper masthead, condensed Anton headlines, electric periwinkle ink, a ruled ledger-table grid, an overprint stamp. Looks printed. Irreverent-premium.
